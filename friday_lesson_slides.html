Water Cycle Guided Notes

Teach Each Stage with Slides:

1. EVAPORATION: Water turns from liquid to gas (water vapor). The Sun heats water.
Gesture: Hands rise up like steam

2. CONDENSATION: Water vapor cools and turns back into liquid. Forms clouds.
Gesture: Hands come together like forming a cloud

3. PRECIPITATION: Water falls from clouds as rain, snow, sleet, or hail.
Gesture: Fingers wiggle down like rain

4. COLLECTION: Water collects in oceans, lakes, rivers, and groundwater.
Gesture: Hands cup together like holding water
